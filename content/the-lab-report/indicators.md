## How We Choose

We don't add indicators blindly. Every indicator in The Lab Report passes three gates:

1. **Reviewed first** — each indicator has a published, in-depth review on The Indicator Lab
2. **Popularity-weighted** — we prioritize indicators traders actually use on TradingView
3. **Signal-tested** — we reject indicators that echo signals already in the consensus (no redundancy)

We add ~5 new indicators every week. [→ Subscribe](/the-lab-report/#pricing) to get them as they go live.
