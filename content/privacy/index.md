## Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

The Indicator Lab ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by GLYC Digital Pte Ltd. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at theindicatorlab.com.

### Information We Collect

**Automatically Collected:**
- **Usage data:** pages visited, time spent, referring site, browser type, device type — collected via our self-hosted Umami analytics. All data is anonymized. No personal identifiers (IP addresses, cookies, or user IDs) are stored.
- **Affiliate clicks:** when you click an affiliate link, we record the click event (page, timestamp, destination) to track commissions.

**Voluntarily Provided:**
- If you contact us via email or our contact form, we retain your message and email address only as long as needed to respond.

### How We Use Your Information

- To operate and improve the website
- To track affiliate link performance (commissions)
- To analyze content popularity via anonymized analytics
- To respond to inquiries

### Analytics

We use **Umami** — a privacy-focused analytics platform self-hosted on our own infrastructure. Umami does **not** use cookies, does **not** collect personal data, and does **not** track you across websites. All analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

### Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This includes links to TradingView, OKX, Moomoo, Coinbase, and other partners.

We only recommend products we have tested and use ourselves. Affiliate relationships do not influence our reviews or ratings.

### Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to third-party websites (TradingView, cryptocurrency exchanges, brokerages, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these sites.

### Data Security

We use Cloudflare for CDN and DDoS protection. All data transmitted between your browser and our site is encrypted via HTTPS. Our analytics server is self-hosted and secured.

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of affiliate click tracking (disable JavaScript in your browser)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: **contact@theindicatorlab.com**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
