## The List

### 1. Atr

**[Atr](/reviews/atr/)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Atr filters out noise effectively, giving you a clean read on trend direction without second-guessing every bar. It works well both as a standalone and paired with momentum for confirmation. Like any trend tool, it lags in fast reversals. You'll give back some profits on sharp turnarounds — that's the trade-off for reliability. Ideal for position traders who hold for weeks and want trend confirmation.

**[Bollinger Bands](/reviews/bollinger-bands/)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)
