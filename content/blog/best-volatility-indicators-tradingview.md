Every trader learns about volatility indicators, but few use them systematically. These tools measure how much a market typically moves, letting you set stops that are tight enough to protect capital but wide enough to survive normal noise.

Our selection spans 10 trend indicators.

To build this list, we filtered our database of 558 reviewed TradingView indicators by the relevant category, then ranked by our star rating system. Each pick was verified for signal consistency, false signal rate, and suitability for the intended use case.

## The List

### 1. Atr

**[Atr](/reviews/atr/)** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5/5)

Atr filters out noise effectively, giving you a clean read on trend direction without second-guessing every bar. It works well both as a standalone and paired with momentum for confirmation. Like any trend tool, it lags in fast reversals. You'll give back some profits on sharp turnarounds — that's the trade-off for reliability. Ideal for position traders who hold for weeks and want trend confirmation.
