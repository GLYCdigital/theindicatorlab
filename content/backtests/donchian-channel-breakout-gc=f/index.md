## How the Donchian Channel Breakout Works

The donchian channel breakout is one of the most widely-used signals in trading. It's simple, mechanical, and removes emotional decision-making from your trading by generating clear entry and exit signals based on the Donchian indicator.

On GC=F, the Donchian acts as a trend filter. It won't catch every exact bottom, but it reliably captures the meat of major moves while keeping you out of chop.

## 5-Year Performance on GC=F

Over five years (2021-07-12 → 2026-07-10), the Donchian Channel Breakout delivered a **+48.7% total return** on GC=F, compounding at **8.3% annually**. 

At **0.44**, the Donchian earned less than one units of return for every unit of risk taken. For context, anything above 1.0 is considered good; above 1.5 is excellent.

The catch? A **23.2% max drawdown**. At some point during this 5-year run, you'd have been down nearly 23% from peak equity. That's significant territory for most traders. If you can't handle that kind of drawdown, this isn't for you — or you need to size down.

The donchian channel breakout shows moderate edge on GC=F — **0.44 Sharpe, 1.73 profit factor** over 67 trades. It's not a home run, but it's also not random.
