## How the Golden Cross Works

The golden cross is one of the most widely-used signals in trading. It's simple, mechanical, and removes emotional decision-making from your trading by generating clear entry and exit signals based on the SMA indicator.

On QQQ, the SMA acts as a trend filter. It won't catch every exact bottom, but it reliably captures the meat of major moves while keeping you out of chop.

## 5-Year Performance on QQQ

Over five years (2021-07-14 → 2026-07-10), the Golden Cross delivered a **+112.7% total return** on QQQ, compounding at **16.3% annually**. 

At **0.81**, the SMA earned less than one units of return for every unit of risk taken. For context, anything above 1.0 is considered good; above 1.5 is excellent.

The catch? A **22.1% max drawdown**. At some point during this 5-year run, you'd have been down nearly 22% from peak equity. That's significant territory for most traders. If you can't handle that kind of drawdown, this isn't for you — or you need to size down.
