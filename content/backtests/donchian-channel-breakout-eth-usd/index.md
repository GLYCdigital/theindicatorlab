## How the Donchian Channel Breakout Works

The donchian channel breakout is one of the most widely-used signals in trading. It's simple, mechanical, and removes emotional decision-making from your trading by generating clear entry and exit signals based on the Donchian indicator.

On ETH, the Donchian acts as a trend filter. It won't catch every exact bottom, but it reliably captures the meat of major moves while keeping you out of chop.

## 5-Year Performance on ETH

Over five years (2021-06-21 → 2026-06-19), the Donchian Channel Breakout delivered a **+72.1% total return** on ETH-USD, compounding at **11.5% annually**. 

At **0.37**, the Donchian earned less than one units of return for every unit of risk taken. For context, anything above 1.0 is considered good; above 1.5 is excellent.

The catch? A **49.8% max drawdown**. At some point during this 5-year run, you'd have been down nearly 50% from peak equity. That's stomach-churning territory for most traders. If you can't handle that kind of drawdown, this isn't for you — or you need to size down.

## Does the Donchian Channel Breakout Work on ETH?

The donchian channel breakout shows moderate edge on ETH — **0.37 Sharpe, 1.13 profit factor** over 93 trades. It's not a home run, but it's also not random.

The 24.7% win rate means you'll be wrong more than half the time. The profit factor above 1.0 means your wins are bigger than your losses, which is what keeps you profitable.
