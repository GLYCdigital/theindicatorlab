## How the Volume Profile Pro Works

The volume profile pro is one of the most widely-used signals in trading. It's simple, mechanical, and removes emotional decision-making from your trading by generating clear entry and exit signals based on the VWAP indicator.

On ETH, the VWAP acts as a trend filter. It won't catch every exact bottom, but it reliably captures the meat of major moves while keeping you out of chop.

## 5-Year Performance on ETH

Over five years (2021-05-29 → 2026-05-26), the Volume Profile Pro delivered a **+135.5% total return** on ETH-USD, compounding at **18.7% annually**. 

At **0.57**, the VWAP earned less than one units of return for every unit of risk taken. For context, anything above 1.0 is considered good; above 1.5 is excellent.

The catch? A **44.5% max drawdown**. At some point during this 5-year run, you'd have been down nearly 44% from peak equity. That's stomach-churning territory for most traders. If you can't handle that kind of drawdown, this isn't for you — or you need to size down.
