## How the TTM Squeeze Pro Works

The ttm squeeze pro is one of the most widely-used signals in trading. It's simple, mechanical, and removes emotional decision-making from your trading by generating clear entry and exit signals based on the BollingerBands indicator.

On TSLA, the BollingerBands acts as a trend filter. It won't catch every exact bottom, but it reliably captures the meat of major moves while keeping you out of chop.

## 5-Year Performance on TSLA

Over five years (2021-05-26 → 2026-05-22), the TTM Squeeze Pro delivered a **+131.5% total return** on TSLA, compounding at **18.3% annually**. 

At **0.53**, the BollingerBands earned less than one units of return for every unit of risk taken. For context, anything above 1.0 is considered good; above 1.5 is excellent.

The catch? A **39.9% max drawdown**. At some point during this 5-year run, you'd have been down nearly 40% from peak equity. That's stomach-churning territory for most traders. If you can't handle that kind of drawdown, this isn't for you — or you need to size down.

## Does the TTM Squeeze Pro Work on TSLA?

The ttm squeeze pro shows moderate edge on TSLA — **0.53 Sharpe, 1.37 profit factor** over 65 trades. It's not a home run, but it's also not random.

The 29.2% win rate means you'll be wrong more than half the time. The profit factor above 1.0 means your wins are bigger than your losses, which is what keeps you profitable.

Our take: usable as a confirmation tool, but not as a standalone system on TSLA. Combine with trend filters or volume confirmation for better results.
