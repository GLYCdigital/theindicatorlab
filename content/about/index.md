## Why We Do This

Most trading tools are sold with hype, not proof. Indicators promise to transform your trading — but nobody tells you whether they actually work. We believe traders deserve better. Real data. Honest reviews. Tools built by people who trade themselves, not marketers who've never placed a trade.

We're tired of the noise. So we built something different.

## How We Do It

We love data. We are obsessed with data analysis. And the only thing we love more is making money in the markets. Combining these together is what drives everything we build — we test every indicator ourselves, run the backtests, and publish the results. The good. The bad. The ugly. No cherry-picking. No marketing fluff.

If an indicator doesn't hold up, we say so. If it does, we explain exactly why — and how to use it.

## What Is This?

The Indicator Lab reviews TradingView indicators so you don't have to waste time guessing which ones are worth installing. Every review is independent, hands-on, and straight to the point.

## How Reviews Work

Each indicator gets scored on:
- **Functionality** — Does it do what it promises?
- **Usability** — How easy is it to set up and tweak?
- **Value** — Is it worth the price (or the screen space if free)?
- **Originality** — Does it actually do something different?
