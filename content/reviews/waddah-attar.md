## Overview

Waddah Attar is a three-in-one indicator: it shows trend direction (EMA), momentum (MACD-based), and volatility (BB width) in a single histogram. When all three align — trend is solid, momentum is building, and volatility is expanding — you get the 'explosive' signal. Traders love it for catching breakouts early.

<!--more-->

## Key Features

- **Triple confirmation** — trend + momentum + volatility in one
- **Explosive signals** — histogram bars turn green when conditions align
- **Dead zone** — red bars warn when conditions aren't right
- **Squeeze detection** — uses BB expansion as volatility trigger
- **Customizable** — three components can be tuned independently

## How to Use

1. Green histogram = explosive conditions — look for entries with the trend
2. Red/dead zone = stay out — conditions aren't aligned
3. Combined with support/resistance zones for breakout confirmations
4. Use on lower timeframes (15m-1H) for quick breakout trades

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- All-in-one indicator — replaces 3 separate tools
- Clear buy/don't-buy signals
- Great for breakout traders and momentum scalpers
- Free on TradingView

**Cons:**
- Complicated internally — black-box feel
- Can give false signals in low-volatility regimes
- Best in trending markets — useless in consolidation
- Three parameters to tune = more optimization risk

## Who Is This For?

- **Breakout traders:** Designed for spotting explosive moves
- **Scalpers:** Fast signals on 5m-15m timeframes
- **Momentum traders:** Clear entry triggers

## Alternatives

- **TTM Squeeze** — Similar concept, more popular
- **Keltner + BB combo** — manual version of the same idea
- **MACD + Bollinger Bands** — DIY approach

Waddah Attar is a fun, effective indicator for catch breakouts. Not every signal hits — but when it does, it's a beauty. For breakout hunters, this is a must-try.
