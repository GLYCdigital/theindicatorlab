Pine3D A Native 3D Graphical Rendering Engine is a groundbreaking TradingView indicator that transforms traditional 2D price charts into a dynamic 3D environment. ## How to Use Pine3D A Native 3D Graphical Rendering Engine

1. Start with default settings: use a 3D view angle of 45 degrees and enable surface transparency at 50% for clarity
1. For scalping: set the rendering resolution to 'Fast' and use a 1-minute timeframe with a 10-period moving average as the 3D base
1. For swing trading: select 'High Quality' rendering with a 4-hour timeframe and a 50-period moving average to visualize long-term trends
1. Combine with traditional indicators like RSI or MACD overlaid on the 3D surface for confirmation
