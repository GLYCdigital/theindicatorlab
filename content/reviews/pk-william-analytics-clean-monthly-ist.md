## What This Indicator Actually Does

This is a **monthly pivot analytics tool** that draws key levels based on the previous month’s open, high, low, and close. It projects support/resistance zones (R1, R2, S1, S2) and includes a clean midline (the monthly pivot). No fancy oscillators, no overlay clutter — just levels that matter for monthly timeframe traders.

What sets it apart from the 50 other monthly pivot indicators on TradingView? **Clean code and zero repaint.** The levels are fixed once the new month starts. I’ve tested this across BTC/USDT, EUR/USD, and TSLA — the lines hold firm and don’t shift mid-week.

## How to Use It for Entries and Exits

This isn’t a “buy on R1, sell on S1” robot. Here’s how I actually trade with it:

**Entry Strategy (Trend Continuation)**  
- Wait for price to break above the monthly pivot (green candle close above).  
- Enter long on a retest of the pivot as support.  
- Target: R1 for partial profit, R2 for full.  
- Stop loss: Below the pivot minus 1 ATR.

**Exit Strategy (Reversal)**  
- If price touches R2 with a bearish divergence on RSI, I take profit or hedge.  
- If price breaks below S1, I look for short entries targeting S2.

**False Break Filter**  
- I only take a break of R1 if the candle closes above it. Intraday wicks don’t count.
