## How It Works

1. **Add to any chart** — market structure lines, blocks, and gaps appear automatically
2. **BOS Lines** — green for bullish breaks, red for bearish. See trend direction at a glance
3. **CHOCH Labels** — early reversal warnings when structure shifts
4. **Order Blocks** — auto-highlighted zones where institutions left orders
5. **FVGs** — unfilled gaps act as price magnets
6. **Sweeps** — flag when price hunts stops through swing levels
7. **Phone Alerts** — 6 conditions: push, email, SMS, or webhook to Discord/Telegram
