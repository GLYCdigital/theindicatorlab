## How It Works

1. **Add to any chart** — anchored VWAP and volume profile appear automatically
2. **VWAP** — the institutional benchmark. Blue line updates with every bar
3. **POC** — yellow line at the price with the most volume. Natural magnet level
4. **Value Area** — 68% zone where two-sided auction happened (VAH/VAL)
5. **Info Table** — live VWAP, POC, VAH, VAL values at a glance
6. **Configurable** — lookback, resolution, price source, position, VA percentage
