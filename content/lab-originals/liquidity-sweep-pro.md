## How It Works

1. **Add to any chart** — equal highs/lows appear as orange dashed lines
2. **Sweeps detected** — when price wicks through a level and closes back inside, "LIQ SWEEP" label appears
3. **MSS confirms** — Market Structure Shift confirms the sweep reversed the trend
4. **Entry zone drawn** — box with stop level based on most recent swing
5. **Phone alerts** — 4 conditions: Sweep Bullish/Bearish, MSS Bullish/Bearish
