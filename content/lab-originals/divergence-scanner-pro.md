## The divergence tool that doesn't just spot one indicator — it cross-references four across three timeframes.

Most divergence tools only check one indicator (usually RSI). Divergence Scanner Pro checks **RSI, MACD, Stochastic, and CCI** simultaneously across **three timeframes** and gives you a **confluence score** so you know how many signals agree.

### What it detects

| Divergence Type | How It Works |
|---|---|
| **Regular Bearish** | Price makes higher high, indicator makes lower high → trend reversal down |
| **Regular Bullish** | Price makes lower low, indicator makes higher low → trend reversal up |
| **Hidden Bearish** | Price makes lower high, indicator makes higher high → continuation down |
| **Hidden Bullish** | Price makes higher low, indicator makes lower low → continuation up |

### Multi-Indicator + Multi-TF

- **RSI** (default 14) — classic momentum divergence
- **MACD** (fast 12 / slow 26) — trend-following divergence
- **Stochastic** (K 14) — overbought/oversold divergence
- **CCI** (20) — commodity channel divergence
- **3 timeframes** — assign each (e.g., current, 1h, 4h)

### Confluence Scoring

Each regular divergence = **base points**. Each hidden divergence = **base points**. Higher timeframe divergences are weighted 3× vs lower timeframe's 1×.

Set a **minimum confluence threshold** (default 3). Only signals above that threshold fire — dramatically reducing false signals compared to single-indicator divergence.

### Dashboard

The info table on your chart shows exactly which indicator×timeframe combinations have divergence active. ⚡ = regular, ◈ = hidden, — = none. You can see at a glance whether the divergence is broad or isolated.

### Alerts

- Bearish divergence (confluence threshold met)
- Bullish divergence (confluence threshold met)
- Customize per indicator/combination
